MetroAir Multicruiser

MULTICRUISER · eVTOL
Technical Overview

Engineered for
Urban Scale

The Multicruiser is MetroAir's flagship eVTOL aircraft, featuring a patented rimfoil structure that encloses each propeller — eliminating visual threat in urban environments while generating wing-like lift at speed. It combines the low-speed maneuverability of a hexacopter with the cruising efficiency of a fixed-wing aircraft.

Company Strategy

MetroAir's Vision

01
The Multi-Cruiser, an electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft, is the company's most important product.
02
Core research focuses on the aerodynamic performance of multi-cruisers and building a 3D transportation system through modularization.
03
Short-term: develop multi-cruiser drones. Long-term: shape a complete urban air vehicle system.
04
Derivatives of the multi-cruiser will be developed, establishing a business model in fields of high added value.
05
We strengthen connections with companies and organizations across various technological fields to build the urban aviation system.
06
We actively attract investment and distribute profits through joint ventures with participating individuals, institutions, and companies.

Commercial Product

MetroAir FPV Drone

First to Market

Bridging Today's
Technology with
Tomorrow's Vision

While MetroAir develops its long-term urban air mobility platform, we are commercializing our FPV drone as the first product to market. Built on the same aerodynamic and propulsion research that powers the Multicruiser, our FPV drone brings MetroAir's engineering expertise to an accessible, high-performance platform.

Revenue and operational data from FPV commercialization directly accelerates our Multicruiser development roadmap.
